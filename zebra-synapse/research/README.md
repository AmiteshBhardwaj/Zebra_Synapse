# Research Archive

This directory stores research, experiments, and provenance material that remains in-repo for traceability. Nothing here is required for Zebra Synapse runtime or standard deployment.

## Contents

- [`ml/`](./ml): offline multimodal rare-disease experimentation
- [`exports/`](./exports): archived research outputs or manifests

## Rules

- Keep research material out of `src/`, `public/`, and `supabase/`.
- Document data sources and reproduction steps inside each research subarea.
- Treat this directory as archive and experiment space, not product dependency.
